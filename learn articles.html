Data Analytics and Menopause

Menopause is when a woman’s ovaries permanently stop producing reproductive hormones for 12 months straight. This event typically happens to women once they reach their late 40’s to early 50’s. Menstrual cycles stop, less estrogen is produced, and physical and mental changes occur in the body. Physical changes include hot flashes, insomnia, night sweats, vaginal dryness, headaches, joint and muscle aches, and weight gain. Mental changes include depression, low libido, memory issues, and irritability.
